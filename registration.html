Cricket Tournament Rules

Rules All Teams Must Follow

  • Team Composition: Each team must consist of 11 players, including a captain. Substitutes are allowed for
    injuries but cannot bat or bowl.
  • Match Format: Matches will follow the Twenty20 format, with each team allowed a maximum of 20 overs to bat.
  • Code of Conduct: Players must adhere to the spirit of the game and maintain sportsmanship at all times. Any
    form of misconduct may lead to penalties or disqualification.
  • Over Limits: A bowler is allowed to bowl a maximum of 4 overs in a match.
  • Fielding Restrictions: Powerplay rules apply, allowing only two fielders outside the 30-yard circle for the
    first 6 overs.
  • No Ball Rules: Front foot no-balls will result in a free hit for the batting team.
  • Match Timings: Teams must report 30 minutes before the scheduled start time. Delays may result in penalties.
  • Equipment: Teams are responsible for their own equipment, including bats, protective gear, and uniforms.
  • Umpire Decisions: Umpire decisions are final and must be respected. Any disputes will be addressed by the
    tournament committee.
  • Rain Interruptions: In case of rain, the Duckworth-Lewis-Stern (DLS) method will be applied to determine the
    result.
